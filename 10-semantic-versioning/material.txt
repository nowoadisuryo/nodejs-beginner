Semantic versioning in Node.js Libraries
Major.Minor.Patch
Example for lodash
lodash: "^4.17.11", it is mean that I want to update lodash when there are a minor and patch update
lodash: "~4.17.11", it is mean that I want to update lodash for only path update
lodash: "4.17.11", it is mean that I want to never update lodash
